S³ as 8 cubes — geodesics in a refractive medium

Each tesseract cell is voxelized N³ ways. A potential φ = Σ 1/(di+ε) (geodesic distance to three poles) gives a refractive index n = eαφ. Geodesics extremize ∫ n ds on S³.
